Lesson 02: Design Principles

Alignment

Family Search

familysearch.org

The landing page of familysearch.org demonstrates great use of the principle of alignment. Starting with the background image which centers on the boy holding a picture, continued through the center aligned text which starts at the center of the page, there is uniformity throughout.

Visual Hierarchy

Nintendo Life

nintendolife.com

Visual hierarchyemphasizes where you should be focusing your attention, and Nintendo Life demonstrates a great case of utilizing that design element here. The most important elements and features are highlighted by the visual emphasis placed on them. For instance, it is easy to see where the featured or highlighted articles are by the size of the picture, and then the next thing you notice visually is the picture for the first article in the latest news section. These are the key features that they want your focus to be on.

White Space

Random-ize

random-ize.com

The Random-ize page is a great example of effectively utilizing white space to create a clean and simple design, without looking like basic html. This page still looks professional with the other elements used, and the simplicity and uncluttered view makes for a nice browsing experience.
