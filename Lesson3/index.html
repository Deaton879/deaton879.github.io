Site Plan

Site Description

The purpose of this site is to be a roadmap for the WeatherTheWeather.app website. This site will cover the purpose and objectives of the WeatherTheWeather website, as well as the target audience. Other vital information pertaining to the website including the basic stucture and design elements are present as well. Ultimately, this site will serve as a rescource throughout the semester for the WDD 230 course project: The Weather Site.

Purpose Statement

The purpose of the final site, WeatherTheWeather.app, will be to provide fast and accurate weather coverage for the public. With the use of this site, the audience will be able to weather any weather by staying up-to-date on the latest weather developments in their area. The site will provide a clean and simple user-interface that is both intuitive and feature rich.
