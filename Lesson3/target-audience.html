Target Audience


Here we will discuss the audience this site is planning to target.


Who

From those who want to know what the weather will be like when they get off work, to the individuals who are monitoring the weather in Hawaii, two month before their vacation.


Age

Estimates put the target audience between the ages of 10 and 110.


Education

The data suggests that the education levels of individuals expected to use this site vary significantly, but that comes as no suprise considering the base of knowledge required to utilize site content.


Motivation

A general necessity to help make key decisions in their everyday life in a fast, convenient, and reliable way.

Scenarios

#1 What makes WeatherTheWeather stand out from the pack?

The simple and pleasant user interface is almost calming, and the forecast is never hidden behind a sea of ads.


#2 What kind of frequency is the information updated?

WTW is an up-to-date rescource that can provide you the most current information available.


#3 Can I use the WTW on my phone or tablet, as well as my computer?

The site has been optimized for various platforms and devices. If you have access to the internet, then you can access WTW site services, guaranteed!


#4 How do I find out what the weather is in my area?

You can access your local weather by using the search functionality provided. Location detection services will soon begin testing to make finding the information you're looking for even more easily accessible.


#5 Is there some kind of fee to use the service?

Absolutely not! We offer a free service with the goal of providing you with essential information to plan your life. All we ask for in return is your feedback so we can continue to enhance the experience offered here. We're here to WeatherTheWeather together!
