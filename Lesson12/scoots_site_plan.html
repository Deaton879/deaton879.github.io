Overview

Purpose

I build websites and program computers. I spend most of my time hopping around multiple webpages for work. If your work is web-based, you might agree that it can be difficult staying productive when you’re busy juggling tabs and webpages. Have you ever felt like there wasn’t enough space on your screen, or like you needed more screens to efficently work? Does navigating your virtual workspace wear you out more than doing your actual work? There are programs that make things like editing photos, or doing taxes super simple by organizing everything you need, all in one place. My website offers that kind of organization and simplicity to your web-based workspace; giving you access to everything you need without the headache of trying to fit, juggle, and resize multiple windows of content into the four corners of your screen, no matter the task.

Audience

My audience consists of those who use web-based applications for work, who are seeking a higher level of productivity/efficiency while working, or for anyone who would like to better organize their web-based workspace. This would include professionals in web-development, students (especially online-only students), or anyone who finds their power to uniquely organize their personal browsing experience lacking. Whether the project is for school, for work, or something personal, the audience for this site is for who are looking for a better way to get things done.

Typography

Heading Font: [Roboto Mono(Bold Italic)]

The Roboto font family is used all over the web. The characters are easily discernable, and prolonged reading of this font is easy on the eyes. This is optimal for a workspace enviroment and it promotes the overall objective/purpose of the site.

The Heading is Roboto Mono: Bold and Italic. This is a good contrast to the text body or paragraph font.

Paragraph Font: [Roboto Condensed (Regular)]

The paragraph body is composed of Roboto: regular. This is a very readable text that does not disctract and does not strain the eyes during prolonged work sessions.

Content

Home page

If your work is web-based, you might agree that it can be difficult staying productive when you’re busy juggling tabs and webpages. Have you ever felt like there wasn’t enough space on your screen, or like you needed more screens to efficently work? Does navigating your virtual workspace wear you out more than doing your actual work? This website offers you the kind of organization and simplicity necessary to take your work-flow to the next level. We give you access to everything you need without the headache of trying to fit, juggle, and resize multiple windows of content into the four corners of your screen, no matter the task.

If you are a professional in web-development, a student, or anyone who finds their power to uniquely organize their personal browsing experience lacking, this is the site for you. Whether the project is for school, for work, or something personal, this site is for those looking for a better way to get things done.

This site is still under-construction, so what you are seeing now is Phase One: The launch site.

Does this look like you?

If your workstation setup looks something like this, you've probably grown accustomed to having a high level of customization, efficiency, and freedom in how you work. Trying to take care of all that one one screen while away from you dedicated setup can become frustrating, to say the least.

With tools that free up your screen to only what you want displayed makes a world of difference. With smart tools that not only let you customize what is visible, but also how those elements look (i.e. shape, size, formatting) and how they function: minimize, maximize, simplify, all on-the-fly to make the most out of the limit real estate that your screen affords. No need to fumble with bookmark bars, simply import your bookmarks from your web browser and gain full access without uneccesary obstruction. We are working on the same sort of control for managment of your tabs, web-search functionality, mailbox access, as well as text editors with IDE functionality, and more!

Its not about how much screen you have, it's about how you use what is there. Like using fullscreen mode, but with all the portals you want to see simultaneously there together, and in whatever configuration, without unecessary frames bars, etc. cluttering the content you specifically want to use and see.

[About Us]

This is us! I program, I code, I do just about all of my work on a computer, and the majority of that work is done in a browser. The frustrations that come from being an online student, from working within a browser day after day, led to me creating this site.

I wanted a better way to coordinate all of the different pages, tabs, and tasks that I found myself juggling on a daily basis. Trying to organize my screen became more exhausting than the actual work (especially since most of my work was done on a laptop, without many opportunities to utilize more than one screen).

The sole purpose of this website is to help people who may struggle the same as I did so that they can become more efficient and productive in their work.
