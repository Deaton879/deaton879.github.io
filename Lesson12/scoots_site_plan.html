Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to promote the rental services of the Scoots motor scooter rental company on the island of Cozumel. Site content is targeted at customers that like to do their homework before getting to the island, and plans to provide information about the services, requirements, and rental process. The website aims to embody the fun and carefree attitude of the island, advertising the ability to escape the droves of tourists found on the west side by exploring the vast coasts and inlets to the south and east.

Audience

This owner-operator company caters to locals, walk-in tourists, enthusiasts, and cruise-based touring groups and partnerships.

Typography

Heading Font: [Merienda One]

The headings are using the font-family: Merienda One.

"'Merienda' is a Spanish term for "afternoon snack", and this pleasant time of the day was worth its own typography.
Merienda has soft shapes, is slightly condensed, and has a rhythm which is an invitation to read short pieces of text. It is ideal for headlines which call for height, as its strokes resemble those of a brush and deliver freshness and a dynamic touch in the development of words."

Paragraph Font: [Quicksand]

The paragraph body is composed of: Quicksand. This is a very readable text that does not disctract and does not strain the eyes.
